There are seven lamps on it,+ yes, seven, and the lamps, which are at the top of it, have seven pipes.3  And next to it are two olive trees,+ one on the right of the bowl and one on the left.”4  Then I asked the angel who was speaking with me: “What do these things mean, my lord?”5  So the angel who was speaking with me asked: “Do you not know what these things mean?”I replied: “No, my lord.”6  He then said to me: “This is the word of Jehovah to Ze·rub′ba·bel: ‘“Not by a military force, nor by power,+ but by my spirit,”+ says Jehovah of armies.7  Who are you, O great mountain?
