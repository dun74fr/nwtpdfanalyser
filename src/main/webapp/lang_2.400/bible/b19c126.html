A Song of the Ascents.126  When Jehovah gathered back the captives of Zion,+We thought we were dreaming. 2  At that time our mouth was filled with laughterAnd our tongue with a joyful shout.+At that time they said among the nations:“Jehovah has done great things for them.”+ 3  Jehovah has done great things for us,+And we are overjoyed. 4  Do gather back* our captives, O Jehovah,Like streams in the Neg′eb.* 5  Those sowing seed with tearsWill reap with a joyful shout. 6  The one who does go out, though weeping,Carrying his bag of seed,Will surely return with a joyful shout,+Carrying in his sheaves.+
