Stand still!”But no one is turning back.+ 9  Plunder silver, plunder gold!There is no end to the treasures.It is stocked with all sorts of precious things.10  The city is empty, desolate, devastated!+Their hearts melt in fear, their knees buckle, their hips tremble;All their faces are flushed.11  Where is the lair of lions,+ where the young lions* feed,Where the lion goes out leading its cub,With no one to make them afraid?12  The lion tore apart enough prey for his cubsAnd strangled for his lionesses.He kept his dens filled with prey,His lairs with torn animals.13  “Look!
