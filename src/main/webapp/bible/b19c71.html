O Sovereign Lord Jehovah;I have trusted in you* since my youth.+ 6  I have depended on you from birth;It was you who took me from my mother’s womb.+I praise you constantly. 7  I have become like a miracle to many,But you are my strong refuge. 8  My mouth is filled with your praise;+All day long I tell about your splendor. 9  Do not cast me off in my old age;+Do not abandon me when my strength
